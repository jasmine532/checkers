how to play

1
the board
The board is 8×8. Pieces only ever live on the dark squares and the light squares are decoration. You play as red or white. Each side starts with 12 pieces filling the first three rows of dark squares.
starting position
2
basic movement
On your turn, click a piece to select it. Then click a dark square diagonally in front of it to move. Regular pieces can only move forward (toward the opponent's side).
selected red piece —> dots show valid moves
3
jumping & capturing
If an opponent's piece is diagonally adjacent and the square beyond it is empty, you must jump over it and captured it (removed from the board).

Captures are mandatory. If you can jump, you have to. You can't ignore a capture to make a regular move.

x If you can jump consecutively, you must do it.
red can jump white —> white piece will be captured
4
king pieces
When your piece reaches the far end of the board, it becomes a king and is marked with a crown ♛. Kings can move and jump in all four diagonal directions, making them much more powerful.
a king —> can move forward and backward
5
winning the game
You win by either capturing all of your opponent's pieces, or leaving them with no legal moves.
